About smartcom

Unmatched Performance – Exceptional Quality

Smartcom Design & Solution is one of the reputed manufacturers of Antenna and RF products.
Smartcom is a team of professionals are specialized in design, development and manufacturing of standard and customized Antenna & RF solution for Telecom, Defense and Space industry. We are part of Make in India initiative and majority of input materials for manufacturing of our products are being locally sourced.
The Smartcom is an antenna systems provider that delivers high-performance solutions based on advanced materials, proprietary design principles and RF system expertise.
Our mission is to solve difficult antenna system issues for existing and next generation wireless standards.
We provide standard and customized solutions to leading OEMs and ODMs for Public Safety, Aviation, Defence/military, SATCOM, WiFi, GPS, Cellular and other wireless technologies.
We utilize latest software for design and manufacturing process. Smartcom designed and supplied various customized Antenna products as per customer required specifications within shortest turnaround time.
Consistent performance and quality have been paramount for the system delivered and focus remains on solid engineering and reliability. Our products frequency range covers from HF, VHF, UHF, Cellular, L Band, S Band and our manufacturing facility located at Bangalore.
